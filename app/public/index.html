Get Started

Notes
Create, organize, and read structured markdown notes. Scan your notes into the app for an auto-generated table of contents, code syntax highlighting, and fast in-page navigation. Use these notes as the source to build flashcards and assemble targeted practice tests.
Flashcards
Study curated decks or import your own via JSON. Choose Term → Definition or Definition → Term modes, flip with keyboard, shuffle, and combine multiple decks into a single session. Use the built‑in prompt helper to turn your source material into clean flashcard JSON in seconds.
Tests
Build practice tests from one or more categories or import full question banks via JSON. Answer with instant feedback, view explanations and difficulty, and track your score across a custom mix of subtopics to focus your study time where it matters most.
